a) Ten miles
b) are
c) a long distance to walk
d) on foot
Answer: b) are
Explanation: When a plural noun denotes a single unit (like a distance), it takes a singular verb. The correct verb is "is."
69. Which of the following is the correct sequence to form a coherent paragraph?(Sentence Rearrangement)NVS 2022
P. The internet has made this easier than ever before.
Q. It allows us to connect with people across the globe instantly.
R. Communication is fundamental to human relationships.
S. Platforms like email and social media have bridged geographical gaps.
a) R-P-Q-S
b) P-Q-R-S
c) Q-S-R-P
d) S-P-R-Q
Answer: a) R-P-Q-S
Explanation: R states the general importance of communication. P introduces the internet's role. Q explains how the internet helps. S provides specific platforms.
70. The patient is feeling __________ better today.(Choose the correct adverb to fill in the blank.)KVS 2021
a) much

Answer: a) much
Explanation: "Much" is used to modify the comparative degree of adjectives and adverbs, like "better."
71. Identify the error in the following sentence:(Error Correction)EMRS 2023
The secretary and treasurer have been asked to submit the report.
a) The secretary and treasurer
b) have been
c) asked to submit
d) the report
Answer: b) have been
Explanation: When two nouns are joined by "and" but refer to the same person, a singular verb is used. Here, "secretary and treasurer" is one person. The correct verb is "has been."

75. Which of the following is the correct sequence to form a coherent paragraph?(Sentence Rearrangement)EMRS 2021

S. This is followed by earthwork and the preparation of the sub-base.
a) Q-P-S-R
b) P-S-Q-R
c) R-P-Q-S

77. Identify the error in the following sentence:(Error Correction)NVS 2023
He is the same man who we met at the conference last month.
a) He is the same man
b) who we met
c) at the conference
d) last month
Answer: b) who we met
Explanation: When the relative pronoun is the object of the verb, "whom" should be used. The correct phrase is "whom we met."
